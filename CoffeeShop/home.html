Why Coffee Shop?

We are who we are: an amazing, slightly crazy team. We conquer the world with a new vision of a coffee shop - as a creative space. We spread the culture of Specialty Coffee and inspire our Guests and Partners.

We want to create a new model of a coffee shop - a coffee shop as a creative space. Our establishments are not just places where Specialty Coffee is served. Our creative coffee shops inspire and change for the better our Team, our Guests and Partners. They become more open and sincere, more cohesive and creative, they become simpler and closer to nature.
